Terms and Conditions

Welcome to the photography Contest inside P A College of Engineering campus! Follow the rules below to participate:

  • Eligibility:

    Open to all P A College students and staff. Students from all years and branches are welcome to participate.

  • Submission Deadline:

    Submit your photos by 10 November 2023 Late entries will not be accepted, so make sure to meet the deadline.

  • Location:

    Capture images within the college campus. You can explore any area of the campus, but please respect the campus boundaries and guidelines.

  • Submission Fee:

    A submission fee of 19 INR is required for each entry. Payment instructions will be provided by the event organizers. Please ensure that you have paid the fee for each photograph you submit.

  • Originality:

    All entries must be original and solely created by the participant. Plagiarism or using someone else's work will result in disqualification.

  • Content:

    The photographs can capture any aspect of the campus â€“ its architecture, natural beauty, events, or unique features. However, the content must be appropriate and in good taste.

  • Photo Format:

    Photos should be submitted in a digital format (JPEG or PNG). Ensure that your photos meet the required format.

  • Post-Processing:

    Basic post-processing like cropping and color correction is allowed, but heavy manipulation that distorts the original image is discouraged. Please maintain the authenticity of your photographs.

  • Model Releases:

    If your photograph includes recognizable individuals, you must obtain their consent for the image to be used in the competition. Respect the privacy and rights of individuals in your photos.

  • Privacy and Respect:

    Ensure that privacy and respect for others are maintained in your photographs. Avoid capturing individuals in compromising situations without their consent.

  • Judging:

    Photographs will be judged based on creativity, composition, originality, and overall quality. The decision of the judging panel is final. Be creative and put your best foot forward.

  • Prizes:

    The best photographs will be rewarded with cash prizes, which will be announced in advance. Exciting rewards await the winners.

  • Usage Rights:

    By participating, you agree that P A College of Engineering may use the submitted photographs for promotional purposes, giving appropriate credit to the photographer. Your work may be showcased to a wider audience.

  • Entry Fee:

    There is a submission fee of 19 INR for each entry. Make sure to follow the payment instructions provided by the organizers to complete your submission.
